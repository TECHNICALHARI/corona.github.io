ABOUT COVID-19

What is COVID-19 (Corona-Virus)

COVID-19 is the infection disease caused by the most recently discovered coronavirus.This new virus and desease were unknown before the outbreak begain in Wuhan,China,in 2019.

Coronaviruses are a large family of viruses which may cause illness inanimals or humans.In humans, several coronavirus are known to cause respiratory infections ranging from the common cold to more severe acute respiratory syndrome(SARS).The most recently discovered coronavirus causes coronavirus disease COVID-19.
